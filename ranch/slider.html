Gallery


Experience the beauty of nature as you explore our vast acres of pristine land. Our gallery offers a glimpse into authentic ranch experiences and the rich traditions of the American West.

The Bounty of Nature

Fruits are nature's sweet treasures. They offer a symphony of flavors, colors, and textures that delight the senses and nourish the body. From succulent berries to juicy citrus, the world of fruits is as diverse as it is delicious.

Starry Nights

As the sun sets and darkness descends, campers can gather around a crackling campfire beneath a canopy of stars. Camping on a farm provides the perfect backdrop for creating cherished memories.

Bonding with Animals

For animal lovers, a farm offers the chance to forge connections with a menagerie of creatures. From friendly goats and curious pigs to majestic horses and playful dogs, interacting with farm animals can be a joyous and heartwarming experience.

Outdoor Recreation

With vast expanses of land to explore, Delos provides ample opportunities for outdoor recreation and adventure. Whether it's fishing in a tranquil pond, kayaking down a lazy river, or embarking on a nature hike through scenic trails, you can rejuvenate your body and invigorate the spirit.

City Slicker to Cowhand

Engaging workshops allow participants to roll up their sleeves and actively participate in farm activities. Whether it's milking cows, gathering eggs, planting seeds, or tending to crops, hands-on experiences provide a deeper understanding of the labor and skills involved in farming.

Campfire Gatherings and Storytelling

As night falls and the stars emerge, gathering around a crackling campfire becomes a cherished tradition. Sharing stories, singing songs, and roasting marshmallows under the canopy of stars foster a sense of camaraderie and connection among campers.

Versatility Meets Flavor

Whether you're craving salmon, trout, tilapia, or something else entirely, we have a diverse selection of fresh fish to choose from. Our farm-to-table approach means you can enjoy delicious seafood that's harvested and delivered with care.

Embracing Nature's Serenity

Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of the countryside. Far away from the noise and distractions of urban living, campers can awaken to the symphony of chirping birds, rustling leaves, and the gentle breeze sweeping across vast fields.

Farm-to-Table Dining

Farm vacations offer a culinary experience like no other, with farm-fresh ingredients taking center stage in delicious meals prepared on-site. Our farm-to-table cooking demonstrations and tasting sessions can further showcase the delicious flavors of locally sourced ingredients.

Immersive Farm Experience

Dive into the heart of agricultural life with guided farm tours. This allows you to learn about the daily operations, sustainable farming practices, and the importance of environmental stewardship.

Star Gazing

Amidst the hustle and bustle of farm activities, you can also find time to relax and unwind. Cozy accommodations such as rustic cabins or tents provide comfortable retreats for restful nights under the stars.

Workshops and Demonstrations

Participate in educational workshops and demonstrations that provide insights into various aspects of farm life and sustainable agriculture. From composting and organic gardening to beekeeping and cheese making, these workshops offer valuable learning opportunities for children and adults alike.

Outdoor Adventures

Nature walks along scenic trails offer opportunities for birdwatching, wildlife spotting, and exploring diverse ecosystems. Families can also enjoy recreational activities such as fishing in ponds, swimming in natural springs, or simply picnicking in picturesque settings.
