Upcoming Projects


These projects aim to diversify the farm’s offerings, enhance sustainability, and create new revenue streams.

What the Future Holds for Delos Ranch


Over the years, Delos has evolved and expanded, adapting to the changing needs of its customers and the community it serves. From its humble beginnings as a small family farm, it has grown into a vibrant and thriving ranch, renowned for its commitment to fostering a culture of learning, innovation, and inclusivity.

Agri-tourism and Education


Create an agri-tourism experience that includes guided tours, educational workshops, and hands-on activities for visitors to learn about sustainable farming practices.



Aquaponics System Expansion


Expand the aquaponics system to increase the production of tilapia and salmon while using the fish waste to fertilize a greater variety of vegetables and herbs.



Organic Cereal Initiative


Launch a line of organic cereals using non-GMO seeds and sustainable farming practices to cater to health-conscious consumers.



Farm-to-Table Restaurant


Open a farm-to-table restaurant on the premises that serves dishes made from the farm’s own produce, offering a unique dining experience.



Equestrian Therapy Program


Develop an equestrian therapy program that utilizes the farm’s horses to provide therapeutic riding sessions for individuals with special needs.



Renewable Energy Project


Install solar panels or wind turbines to power the farm operations, reducing the carbon footprint and potentially generating additional income through energy sales.



Cattle Breed Improvement


Implement a selective breeding program to improve the quality of the cattle stock, focusing on traits like disease resistance and meat quality.



The Future Seems Bright


Over the years, Delos has evolved and expanded, adapting to the changing needs of its customers and the community it serves. From its humble beginnings as a small family farm, it has grown into a vibrant and thriving ranch, renowned for its commitment to fostering a culture of learning, innovation, and inclusivity. As we look towards the future, we remain steadfast in our commitment to quality, striving to uphold our legacy of authenticity and continue inspiring and empowering generations for years to come.
