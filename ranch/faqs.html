Frequently Asked Questions


As advocates for sustainable aquaculture, we are committed to giving back to our community. Through educational initiatives, outreach programs, and partnerships with local organizations, we strive to raise awareness about responsible seafood consumption and environmental stewardship.

What products does Delos offer?
Delos Ranch and Farm offers a wide variety of fresh produce. This includes a diverse range of fruits and vegetables, ensuring customers have access to nutritious and fresh food options. We also stock a selection of grains and soya products. These are essential food items in many households. Customers can get most of their grocery shopping done in one place.
What farming methods does Delos use?
We are committed to sustainable and natural farming methods. We understand the importance of preserving the environment and ensuring the health and safety of our customers. Therefore, we employ farming practices that are not only beneficial for the produce but also have minimal impact on the environment. These methods include using natural fertilizers and pest control methods, practicing crop rotation to maintain soil health, and conserving water. We provide fresh and healthy produce while also contributing to environmental sustainability.
Does Delos use any growth hormones or antibiotics?
No, we are committed to providing our customers with safe and healthy food products. As such, we ensure that our products are free from antibiotics or growth hormones. These substances are often used in conventional farming to promote growth and prevent disease, but they can have negative effects on human health and the environment. By avoiding these substances, we ensure that our products are as natural and safe as possible.
Is Delos Ranch open to the public for visits or tours?
Yes, Delos Ranch and Farm is open to the public for visits and tours. We believe in transparency and want our customers to see firsthand the care and dedication that goes into producing the food they consume. During these visits, customers can learn about the farming methods used, meet the people behind the products, and even get a chance to see the crops growing in the fields. This not only provides an educational experience but also helps build trust and connection with our customers.
Does Delos Ranch offer online ordering or delivery services?
Yes, understanding the need for convenience in today’s fast-paced world, we offer online ordering and delivery services. This allows customers to shop for their favorite products from the comfort of their homes and have them delivered straight to their doorsteps. This service is particularly beneficial for those who may not have the time to visit us in person or those who live farther away.
Does Delos Ranch and Farm offer horse riding activities?
Yes, in addition to our farming operations, we also offers horse riding activities. This provides a unique and exciting experience for visitors, allowing them to explore the beautiful landscapes of the farm while enjoying a fun and engaging activity. Whether you’re an experienced rider or a beginner, our trainers ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all.
