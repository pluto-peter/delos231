Trout


Delos is proud to present our exquisite selection of trout, meticulously raised in the pristine waters of Mt Kenya. Renowned for their delicate flavor, tender texture, and exceptional nutritional value, our trout embody the essence of fine aquaculture.

Uncompromising Quality Assurance


Quality is our top priority, and our trout undergo rigorous quality assurance measures at every stage of their lifecycle. From the moment they hatch to the time they're harvested, our fish are closely monitored and cared for by our expert team. We maintain strict hygiene standards and utilize advanced technology to guarantee the freshness, flavor, and nutritional integrity of our trout.


Trout's mild, slightly sweet flavor and firm, flaky texture make it a versatile culinary canvas. Whether grilled, baked, fried, or incorporated into a variety of dishes, our trout delights the palate with its delicate taste and satisfying texture. With its high protein content and low fat profile, tilapia is not only delicious but also a nutritious choice for health-conscious consumers. As advocates for sustainable aquaculture, we are committed to giving back to our community. Through educational initiatives, outreach programs, and partnerships with local organizations, we strive to raise awareness about responsible seafood consumption and environmental stewardship.

Community-Centered Sustainability


Happy fish make for delicious fish. That's why we've created an environment that mirrors the natural habitat of trout, ensuring they thrive in spacious, clean waters. Our commitment to maintaining optimal water quality and environmental balance is reflected in the health and vibrancy of our trout. We believe in responsible farming practices that minimize our environmental footprint.
