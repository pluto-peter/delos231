At Delos, we believe in creating memories that last a lifetime. Indulge your taste buds with delicious farm-to-table cuisine crafted from the freshest locally sourced ingredients. Savor the flavors of the West as you dine under the stars or cozy up by the fireplace in our rustic yet elegant spaces.

Experience the beauty of nature as you explore our vast acres of pristine land. From horseback riding through rolling hills to hiking along scenic trails, immerse yourself in the breathtaking landscapes that surround our ranch.

For those craving a more laid-back retreat, simply unwind by the tranquil waters of our lakes or indulge in a soothing massage at our spa.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a bit of both, our diverse range of activities and amenities ensures there's something for everyone.
